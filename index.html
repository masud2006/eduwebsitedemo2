Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course suitable for beginners?
Absolutely! We have batches for all levels – from basic to advanced. You'll take a placement test before starting.
Can I switch from online to offline?
Yes, you can switch anytime (subject to seat availability). Our counsellors will assist you.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes, all Premium and Intensive plans include job support – resume, mock interviews, and corporate referrals.
What is the class duration?
Each session is 90 minutes. Online batches are 3 days/week, offline 4 days/week (weekend batches available).
